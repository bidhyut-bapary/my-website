Introduction

The Project Management Professional (PMP) certification is one of the most globally recognized credentials for project managers. This course is designed to equip you with the latest tools, techniques, and frameworks required for successful project management in various industries, including IT, construction, finance, and healthcare. It also prepares you for the PMP certification exam offered by the Project Management Institute (PMI).

Course Overview

In this comprehensive PMP course, you will gain in-depth knowledge in all knowledge areas and 5 process groups defined by PMI. The course is structured to cover all quality management areas in project management.

  • Project Initiation & Planning
  • Scope, Schedule, Cost and Quality Management
  • Risk, Resource, Communication, Procurement Management
  • Agile & Hybrid Project Management
  • Stakeholder and Integration Management

This course follows the PMBOK Guide (6th edition), with real-life case studies suited for Bangladeshi professionals and global clients.
